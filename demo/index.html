Getting Started

Get started in seconds. Just add the script to your page:

<script src="path/to/spotlight.min.js"></script>

That's it! Spotlight automatically initializes and instantly enhances your images.

Gallery Mode

Effortless galleries. Just wrap your images in a container with the .gallery class, and Spotlight will automatically group them into a seamless collection.

<div class="gallery">
    <img src="1.webp" alt="Fly agaric mushrooms">
    <img src="2.webp" alt="Pink water lilies">
    <img src="3.webp" alt="Crabapple blossoms">
</div>

Article Mode

Perfect for content. Spotlight automatically detects images inside <article> tags, intelligently pairing them with captions from figcaption or alt attributes.

Moscow Landmarks

The Spasskaya Tower is the main tower on the eastern wall of the Moscow Kremlin which overlooks the Red Square.

Another iconic location is VDNKh, a permanent general purpose trade show and amusement park in Moscow.

<article>
    <h3>Moscow Landmarks</h3>
    <p>...</p>
    <img src="4.webp" alt="Spasskaya Tower">
    <p>...</p>
    <figure>
        <img src="5.webp" alt="VDNKh Park, Moscow">
        <figcaption>Friendship of the Peoples Fountain.</figcaption>
    </figure>
</article>

Features

Smart Grouping

Auto-magically groups images in galleries and articles. No configuration required.

Captions Support

Automatically displays captions from figcaption or alt attributes.

Smooth Animations

Butter-smooth, hardware-accelerated transitions that feel native on every device.

Multi-Input

Navigate your way. Full support for Keyboard, Trackpad, and Touch gestures.

Zero Dependencies

Ultra-lightweight and bloat-free. No jQuery or external libraries needed.

Accessibility

Inclusive by design. Built with semantic HTML and full keyboard navigation support.
